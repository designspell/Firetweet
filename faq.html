FAQ:

 

   1. Is 'FireTweet' a product or a service?

      ANS: 'FireTweet' offers subscription based SERVICES to its customer. We do not sell our hardware devices or software.

 

   2. What is covered in 'FireTweet' service?

       ANS: We currently offer to monitor your fire safety systems and equipments such as Fire Hydrant system, Fire Sprinkler system, Fire Extinguishers and Fire Alarm System. We are planning and working on to expand the scope of our services in Fire Safety monitoring and related areas.

 

    3. How do I benefit from the 'FireTweet' service?

       ANS: By availing 'FireTweet' services, the customer gets a piece of mind that their Fire Safety systems and equipments are fully prepared in the event of fire. Our Central Monitoring Station keeps a watch on your Fire Safety systems and equipments over the clock and resolves the issues, if any, at the earliest by contacting your site staff or vendor contacts.

 

     4. What happens when I plan to avail the services from 'FireTweet'?

       ANS: Once the service is availed, our audit and installation team will visit your site and check your fire fighting systems and equipments that needs to be monitored. Once the audit is completed, we will provide you the quote. Once accepted, our installation team will install and commission our devices at your site. This generally takes single visit but can extend over multiple visits. Our Central Monitoring Station will then start monitoring the health of your fire safety systems and equipments. In case of any problem, they will call the contact person and resolve the issue. Also the customer will be given access to FireTweet portal and mobile application to its key personnel to monitor the real time events related to monitored fire safety systems and equipments.

 

   5. How to buy services from 'Firetweet'?

       ANS: Please contact us on our telephone numbers given on 'Contact Us' page or drop us an enquiry by submitting the enquiry form on this website. Our representative will contact you to understand your needs and explain you the procedure to avail our services.

 

   6. What happens when the subscription ends?

       ANS: 'FireTweet' offers the extension of the services by paying further subscription fees before the expiry date. If customer decides to discontinue the services, then FireTweet will remove and take back all its hardware devices and discontinue monitoring.
